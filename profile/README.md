# intcube · Unconsulting cybersecurity. At scale.

Welcome to the GitHub home of **intcube**, where we share some of our code and track changes to projects we use/work with upstream. Internally, we build automation for "unconsulting" – turning repeatable cybersecurity consulting work into scalable, productized tools. 

---

## What we do

- Pragmatic cybersecurity for investors and mid-sized companies  
- Turning manual consulting processes into automations and products ("unconsulting")  
- Building  tools that support real projects and cohorts in the field 

---

## Roadmapp3r: our flagship tool

Roadmapp3r is our primary (proprietary) product in this space: an automation tool that supports our cohorts and client projects by turning scattered security insights into an actionable, transparent roadmap. 

At a high level, Roadmapp3r helps you:
- Prioritize actions across people, process, and technology  
- Create a living roadmap that can be shared with teams and stakeholders  
- Reduce the "consultant dependency" by making assumptions and decisions explicit
- Gain insights into scattered company structures or portfolios
